Editorial Standards

StartMyLoveEngine is a trust-first editorial platform. Inclusion is guided by quality, partner fit, and user value, not by pay-to-publish dynamics.

Core principles

1. Quality before visibility. Profiles and stories must meet baseline standards for clarity, professionalism, and relevance.

2. Editorial independence. Commercial relationships do not override editorial decisions.

3. Accuracy and transparency. Listings should reflect current details and honest positioning.

4. Respectful representation. We prioritize inclusive storytelling and professional conduct.

Review process

Every profile and editorial opportunity is reviewed for alignment with platform standards. Visibility may be limited, revised, or removed when standards are not maintained.

Scope

These standards apply to directory listings, editorial features, and partnership materials across StartMyLoveEngine.
