Venue Partnership, Built in Public

StartMyLoveEngine is live in pre-launch. We are opening founder conversations with a small number of design-led venues that care about trust, editorial quality and long-term brand equity.

Why venues speak with us now

  1. Not a marketplace model

    We do not sell bulk lead volume or ranking shortcuts. The model is built for fewer, better-fit introductions grounded in context.

  2. Editorial context before enquiry

    Couples should understand your venue before they ever reach out. Our approach combines profile clarity with editorial framing that protects premium positioning.

  3. Direct founder access

    In pre-launch, you speak directly with the founder on fit, standards and rollout priorities. No support queue, no sales script.

How partnership works (pre-launch)

  1. Fit conversation first

    We start with positioning: your venue identity, the couples you want to attract, and whether StartMyLoveEngine is the right channel.

  2. Standards before exposure

    Visibility only expands when profile quality, accuracy and brand fit meet our baseline. Trust is protected on both sides.

  3. Editorial-led visibility

    Where there is clear alignment, editorial features help explain venue value in a way listings alone cannot.

Non-negotiables

  • Commercial participation does not override editorial or quality standards.
  • Profiles are reviewed for fit and accuracy before broader visibility.
  • Editorial inclusion follows story merit and alignment, not pay-to-publish dynamics.

What is live now

  • StartMyLoveEngine is live in pre-launch and founder partnership conversations are open now.
  • Sun Ranch Byron is currently the only live venue profile and is presently unclaimed while full venue profile buildout continues.
  • A live editorial feature set at Sun Ranch Byron is published in The Edit.
