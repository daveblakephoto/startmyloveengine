About Dave

Modern Romance, Editorial Style

I photograph couples who love fashion-led imagery, artful ideas, and the freedom to elope or celebrate without rules.

Dave Blake is a fashion photographer & filmmaker based in Brisbane & Byron Bay, available worldwide for intimate celebrations.

After more than a decade shooting fashion models & campaigns across Australia, New York, and Paris, I founded StartMyLoveEngine (SM(I)LE) to focus on what started to move me: Creating aspirational, fashion-forward editorial imagery for couples that appreciate timeless romance.

These are the moments that last.

My background in editorial photography & film production gives me the tools to create work that feels polished while staying deeply personal, so you can be fully present on the day.
