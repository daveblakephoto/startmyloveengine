About Dave

Heart-led, Editorial Storytelling

My take on couples who love fashion & fragrance–inspired imagery, artful ideas, and the freedom to elope or keep the wedding small without the rules.

Dave Blake is a fashion photographer & filmmaker based in Brisbane & Byron Bay, travelling wherever your story leads.

After more than a decade shooting models & campaigns across Australia, New York, and Paris, I founded StartMyLoveEngine (SM(I)LE) to focus on what started to move me: the unscripted, intimate moments – soft glances, quiet laughter, golden hour promises.

These are the moments that last.

My background in editorial photography & film production gives me the tools to create work that feels polished while staying deeply personal. I work hands-on with every couple – from location scouting and timeline planning to custom film direction – so when the day arrives you can be fully present.

Frequently Asked Questions
