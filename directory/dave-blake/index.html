About Dave

Modern Romance, Editorial Style

Dave Blake is a fashion photographer & filmmaker based in Brisbane & Byron Bay, available worldwide for couples who do things differently.

I photograph couples who love fashion-led imagery, artful ideas, and the freedom to elope or celebrate without rules.

After more than a decade shooting models & fashion campaigns across Australia, New York, and Paris, I founded StartMyLoveEngine (SM(I)LE) to focus on what started to move me: Creating aspirational, fashion-forward editorial imagery for modern romantics.

These are the moments that last.

My background in editorial photography & film production gives me the tools to create work that feels polished while staying deeply personal, so you can be fully present on the day.

What Shapes The Experience

Clear Direction, Zero Awkwardness

You're never left wondering what to do. I give confident, subtle direction so everything feels natural and looks elevated without feeling forced. No stiff posing, no cringe moments, just clean editorial energy.

Fashion-First Eye

With over a decade shooting campaigns across Australia, New York and Paris, I bring a trained editorial eye to every frame. Composition, light and styling all matter. Your images are crafted, not just captured.

Fast Turnaround, Pro-Level Workflow

I work efficiently and deliver quickly. You'll get proofs fast and finals even faster because clear communication and respect for your time are non-negotiable.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where can we work with you?

I split my time between Brisbane and Byron Bay and regularly travel worldwide for elopements and intimate couple experiences. If you have a location in mind, chances are I'm already keen.

Do you shoot traditional weddings?

Not in the conventional sense. I focus on destination elopements, elopements and intimate weddings, engagements, proposals and couple portraits for people who want something more editorial and intentional. If you’re planning a large, traditional wedding, I’m probably not your guy. Don't judge me.

How do you work?

I approach every project the same way I do my fashion work. We start with pre-production call(s) so I can see and understand your creative vision. On the day, I shoot editorial stills and, if you want, a short film on vintage lenses. Proofs fast. Finals faster. Clear comms always.

How do we start?

Send through your date and vision via email. We'll jump on a call to map out a plan.

How much should we budget?
