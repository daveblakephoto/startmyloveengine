About Nahid Kholghi

Elegant. Editorial. Unforgettable.

German-born hair and make-up artist Nahid Kholghi is based in Byron Bay and brings over 15 years of international experience across fashion, editorial and luxury beauty.

Her career spans iconic global brands including Calvin Klein and Shiseido, where she also worked as a creative supervisor and educator. Nahid’s celebrity clientele includes Kylie Minogue, Dannii Minogue, Orlando Bloom, Pierce Brosnan, Katy Perry and Turia Pitt.

Since relocating to Australia in 2017, Nahid has become a sought-after artist for modern brides and editorial weddings, known for her calm presence and refined aesthetic. Her work is effortlessly polished, timeless and fashion-led, creating elevated beauty that photographs beautifully and feels true to each client.
