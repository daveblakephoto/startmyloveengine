The Human Behind the Lens

Dave Blake is a fashion photographer & filmmaker based in Brisbane & Byron Bay, but able to travel wherever your story leads.

After more than a decade shooting models & campaigns across Australia, New York, and Paris, I founded StartMyLoveEngine /SM(I)LE to focus on what started to move me: the unscripted, intimate moments — soft glances, quiet laughter, and golden hour promises.

These are the moments that last. My background in editorial photography & film production gives me the tools to create work that feels polished while staying deeply personal. I work hands-on with every couple from location scouting and timeline planning to custom film direction so that when the day arrives you can be fully present.

Questions Couples Ask

Where can we invite you?

Dave splits time between Brisbane and Byron Bay and regularly travels worldwide for intentional elopements.

What’s included in photo + film coverage?

Every collection is tailored, but expect hands-on planning, location advice, and a blend of editorial stills with a cinematic short film shot on vintage lenses.

How much should we budget for photo & film?

My pricing reflects a fashion industry mindset. Every project is treated as a creative commission. As a guide:

  • Local collections begin at $3,950 AUD for 3.5 hrs of coverage, 35 edited stills, and a 5–6 min cinematic short film.
  • International destination productions typically range from $7,000–$10,000 AUD, plus travel expenses (flights, accommodation, and ground transport), depending on the location, pre-production needs, and number of shoot days.

Every project is bespoke, shaped around your vision, your location, and what matters most to you, with pricing tailored to the scale and story of your day. Quotes are tailored and shared after our initial meeting, so we can align on your vision, location, and what matters most to capture.

How do we start the process?

Share your date and dream landscape via email. You’ll hop on a call with Dave to design a timeline, locations, and creative direction before locking in your experience.

Ready to start?

Share your date, your dream landscape, or simply the feeling you want to remember. I’ll handle the rest and build an experience that feels like the two of you.
